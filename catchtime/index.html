CatchTime · 时间环

把一段时间划分成若干阶段，CatchTime 依据当前时钟自动告诉你现在处于哪个阶段——无需启动计时器，无需手动切换。环形视图让你随时看清整体节奏与当前位置。

Divide any time window into phases and CatchTime shows which one you're in right now — driven entirely by your device clock, with no timer to start or stop. The ring view keeps the full schedule visible at a glance.
